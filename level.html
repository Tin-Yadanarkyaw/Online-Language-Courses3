Thai

There are Six levels in Thai.

Level 1 (Comparable to beginner level),

Level 2 (Comparable to the junior elementary school level),

Level 3 (Comparable to the elementary school level),

Level 4 (Comparable to the junior secondary school level),

Level 5 (Comparable to the secondary school level),

Level 6 (Comparable to a native-level).
